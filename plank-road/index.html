— 04 / The Plank Road

Built in 1800.
Still here.

In the mid-1800s, Wisconsin built a wooden plank road connecting Green Bay to Fond du Lac. It was the state's first real highway. It later became a railway, then the Fox River Trail you can walk today.

We sit just off the original alignment. The pub takes its name and its character from that road. Built to last. Built for everyone passing through.
